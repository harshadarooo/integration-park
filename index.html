Welcome to the National Integration Park

Home

What if a park could change how we see our country… and each other? The National Integration Park, a unique initiative by Aseem Foundation, is not just a space, it’s a movement. Inaugurated on 22nd December 2021 by General MM Naravane, this park invites every visitor to experience India’s spirit through stories, cultures, and connections.

About

A Living Tribute to Bravery
At Thunder Park & Rays of Hope, discover the lives of 21 Param Vir Chakra awardees - India’s bravest. Through augmented reality and sand sculptures, their courage comes alive to remind us what true heroism looks like.

Cultural Center - India in a Circle
Step into huts from across the country, from the houseboats of Kashmir to the living root bridges of Meghalaya. Taste. Touch. Listen. Learn.

Rays Of Hope

In the heart of The National Integration Park lies a place where we remember twenty-one of India's bravest souls - heroes who thought beyond themselves, and with unwavering courage, upheld the integrity of our nation. Welcome to Rays of Hope.
