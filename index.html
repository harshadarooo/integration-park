Welcome to the National Integration Park

Home

What if a park could change how we see our country… and each other? The National Integration Park, a unique initiative by Aseem Foundation, is not just a space, it’s a movement. Inaugurated on 22nd December 2021 by General MM Naravane, this park invites every visitor to experience India’s spirit through stories, cultures, and connections.

About

A Living Tribute to Bravery At Thunder Park & Rays of Hope, discover the lives of 21 Param Vir Chakra awardees - India’s bravest. Through augmented reality and sand sculptures, their courage comes alive to remind us what true heroism looks like. Cultural Center - India in a Circle Step into huts from across the country, from the houseboats of Kashmir to the living root bridges of Meghalaya. Taste. Touch. Listen. Learn. Celebrate India’s diversity in a way that builds understanding and pride. Not Just Exhibits but Real Conversations Our volunteers guide youth through stories of real people, real lands, and real struggles. Assumptions fall away. Respect takes root. This is where curiosity meets connection. This Park is a reflection of the belief that unity can be built by knowing each other better. It’s a space that inspires empathy, patriotism, and purpose. A Gift to the Youth. A Promise to the Nation. Here, heroes are remembered. Here, India breathes as one. Welcome to the National Integration Park. Where learning meets patriotism, and diversity becomes our strength.
